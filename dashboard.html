About


This Personal Time Management App helps users plan and track their daily tasks efficiently. You can create tasks, set deadlines, assign priorities, and mark them as completed. All data is saved using localStorage, so your tasks stay even after you refresh or close the browser. The app features a clean, user-friendly design with options to edit, delete, and filter tasks, making it easy to stay organized and manage your time better.
